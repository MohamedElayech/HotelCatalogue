Discription

Discover the Amber El Fell Hotel

Amber El Fell Hammamet is a very pleasant hotel with its warm welcome and spacious rooms. It has an outdoor swimming pool, a private beach with a 24-hour reception service, a restaurant, a bar and a tennis court.

Location of the Amber El Fell Hotel

Amber El Fell is a 4-star hotel, located in the heart of Hammamet Nord, 700 meters from the Mrezga beach, 6.4 km from the Kasbah of Hammamet and 6.7 km from the Carthageland amusement park. The nearest airport is Enfidha-Hammamet International Airport, 59 km away.

Accommodation at the Amber El Fell Hotel

Amber El Fell Hammamet is a new, fully renovated unit with 61 rooms, most of which offer a sea view. The rooms, whether double, double with lounge (two sofa beds in the same room) or junior suites (two rooms; a master bedroom and a lounge with two sofa beds) are all richly furnished and equipped. The rooms meet the standards of 4-star hotels. This totally independent unit with its own access has a private beach, a private swimming pool, a kitchen and a restaurant with sea view.

Activities and Leisure:

Amber El Fell has a playground for children, a tennis court, and a team of experienced animators.

Free services:

Parking is available on site at the hotel.

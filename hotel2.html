Discover the Fiesta Beach Hotel

The Fiesta Beach Hotel is located in the heart of the tourist area, directly on a beautiful sandy beach. This club is friendly and popular for its festive atmosphere, with a dynamic and very present animation team offering numerous activities.

Location of the Fiesta Beach Hotel

The Fiesta Beach Hotel is located 15 km from Houmt Souk via the Tourist Road. The international airport of Djerba-Zarzis is 25 km away, and it is 10 minutes from Midoun and 7 km from the Fadhloun Mosque.

Accommodation at the Fiesta Beach Hotel

The 354 bungalows and 20 rooms are built in the local style, designed to keep cool, with each room having a bath or shower and a terrace or balcony overlooking the sea, pool, or garden. Spacious and comfortable, they can be transformed into triple or quadruple rooms.

Activities and Leisure:

Many opportunities to practice all land and water sports and various activities during the day. In the evening, enjoy our dance shows, cabarets, folklore... and all night long with the music of our disco.

Free services:

A free parking lot has been set up on site, and car rental is available at the hotel reception. Free wifi is also available.
